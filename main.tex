\begin{cnabstract}

随着可再生能源快速发展，风力发电在现代电力系统中的占比持续提升。精确的风电场超短期发电功率预测可以保障电力系统的安全稳定运行，对风电场的经济调度和运行管理具有重要意义。
本文基于风电场历史发电功率数据和气象数据，研究了深度学习方法在超短期功率预测中的应用。并对比不同深度学习模型在超短期功率预测中的性能。
本文对国家电网发布的新能源功率预测数据中的六个不同地理位置和装机容量（36$\sim$200 MW）的实际风电场的实测数据进行验证。结果表明，在15分钟和30分钟预测（LF=1至LF=2）时，三种模型精度接近，$R^2$均保持在较高水平。随着预测步长增加，不同深度学习模型的误差均有所增加，在步长为2（30分钟）时，三种模型的预测精度接近，预测步长大于2时，CNN-LSTM混合模型的误差最小。


\end{cnabstract}

\cnkeywords{风电功率预测；超短期预测；卷积神经网络；长短期记忆网络；深度学习}

\begin{enabstract}
With the rapid expansion of renewable energy, wind power has claimed an increasingly significant share of modern electricity systems. Accurate ultra-short-term power forecasting for wind farms is essential for ensuring the safe and stable operation of power grids, and carries considerable practical value for economic dispatch and operational management.

This study investigates the application of deep learning methods to ultra-short-term wind power forecasting, drawing on historical generation data and meteorological data from wind farms. The predictive performance of several deep learning models is systematically compared under this forecasting task.

Validation is conducted using measured data from six real-world wind farms with varying geographic locations and installed capacities (36–200 MW), sourced from the renewable energy power forecasting dataset published by State Grid Corporation of China. Results show that at 15-minute and 30-minute horizons (LF = 1 to LF = 2), all three models achieve comparable accuracy, with R² values consistently maintained at high levels. As the forecast horizon extends, prediction errors increase across all models. At a lead time of two steps (30 minutes), the three models perform similarly; beyond that point, the hybrid CNN-LSTM model yields the lowest error.
\end{enabstract}
\enkeywords{Wind Power Forecasting; Ultra-Short-Term Prediction; Convolutional Neural Network; Long Short-Term Memory; Deep Learning}
